# Vermont Data Science Group - Brand Guide

Welcome to the Vermont Data Science Group Brand Guide! This guide provides comprehensive information about our brand identity, including logos, colors, typography, and usage guidelines.

This brand guide is designed to help you understand and use the Vermont Data Science Group brand consistently across all platforms and materials. Whether you're creating presentations, documents, websites, or other materials, this guide will help ensure brand consistency.

- **Colors**: Our brand color palette with hex codes and usage guidelines
- **Typography**: Font families and typographic guidelines
- **Usage Guidelines**: Best practices for applying our brand elements

## Getting Started

Navigate through the sections using the sidebar to explore our brand elements. Each section includes detailed guidelines and examples to help you implement our brand correctly.
