## Clear Space

Always maintain adequate clear space around the logo to ensure visibility and impact. The minimum clear space should be equal to the height of the "V" in the logo on all sides.

## Minimum Size

To maintain legibility, never reproduce the logo smaller than:
- **Print**: 0.5 inches wide
- **Digital**: 72 pixels wide

## What Not to Do

Do not stretch or distort the logo
Do not change the logo colors
Do not rotate the logo
Do not add effects (shadows, gradients, etc.)
Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds
